How it works

Library Role Description
Lustache Presentation renders the template to html: initial like count from the server.
Fixi.js Network Updates sends a POST request to /hype/like, then replaces the inner HTML of #like-count with the server's response without page reload. Dispatches events: fx:before - triggered on an element just before a fetch() request is made. fx:after - triggered on an element just after a fetch() request finishes normally but before content is swapped fx:swapped - triggered after the swap and any associated View Transition has completed.
Hyperscript Behavior listens to Fixi.js events: on fx:before disables the button and scales it down to 0.9; on fx:swapped scales back to 1 and re‑enables the button; on fx:after (from the button) → fades the like counter in/out.


{{likes}} 


<div>
  <span id="like-count"
        _="on fx:after from #like-button
           transition my opacity to 0 over 50ms
           then transition my opacity to 1 over 500ms">
    {{likes}}
  </span>
  <br><br>
  <button id="like-button"
          fx-action="/hype/like"
          fx-method="post"
          fx-target="#like-count"
          fx-swap="innerHTML"
          _="on fx:before from me 
                toggle @disabled on me then
                transition *transform to 'scale(0.9)'
             
             on fx:swapped from me
                transition *transform to 'scale(1)' then
                toggle @disabled on me ">
    Like
  </button>
</div>
